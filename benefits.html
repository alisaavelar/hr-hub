Benefits

Enrollment guidance, open enrollment support, and benefits Q&A — so every employee knows what they have and how to use it.
1 Skill · 5 Sub-pages Cowork Plugin Notion + Drive Linked

Why This Matters

Benefits are one of the most valuable parts of a total comp package — and among the most confusing. Employees often don't fully understand what they have until they need it. This skill makes benefits accessible in plain language, guides people through enrollment decisions, and helps HR answer the same questions without copying from the handbook every time.

What This Skill Does

🏥
Benefits Q&A
Plain-language answers to any benefits question — health, dental, vision, 401(k), FSA/HSA, life insurance, disability, and wellness stipends.
📅
Open Enrollment Support
Help employees compare plan options, understand trade-offs (HMO vs. PPO, HSA eligibility), and make enrollment decisions that fit their situation.
🆕
New Hire Orientation
A structured walkthrough of all available benefits for new employees — what to enroll in, deadlines, and how each benefit works day-to-day.

How to Use This Skill

1
Install the HR Hub plugin
Download the plugin below and install it via Cowork → Settings → Plugins. The benefits skill activates automatically.
2
Open a new Cowork conversation
Start a fresh session. The plugin loads automatically — no need to select it manually.
💡 You can ask in plain English — no special commands needed
3
Ask your benefits question
Type any benefits-related question. The skill will answer in plain language, reference CodePath's specific plans, and link to source documents in Notion and Google Drive when relevant.
4
Follow up or go deeper
Ask follow-up questions, request plan comparisons, or ask the skill to draft an FAQ you can share with employees. It remembers context within the conversation.
📎 Paste in an employee's specific situation for personalized guidance
