🧠 Employee Assistance Program

Free, confidential 24/7 support for mental health, financial stress, legal questions, and more.
100% Confidential 24/7 Access Free to All Employees
🔒
Completely Confidential
EAP usage is never reported to CodePath. HR and management have no visibility into who uses the program or why.

What's Covered

🧠
Mental Health
Counseling sessions, therapy referrals, stress management, anxiety, depression, and work-life balance support.
💰
Financial Counseling
Budgeting help, debt counseling, financial planning guidance, and referrals to financial advisors.
⚖️
Legal Referrals
Free consultations with attorneys for personal legal matters — family law, housing, estate planning, and more.
👨‍👩‍👧
Family Support
Childcare resources, elder care referrals, parenting support, and family crisis counseling.
🏢
Work-Related Issues
Conflict resolution, workplace stress, burnout prevention, and career transition support.
🌙
Crisis Support
24/7 crisis hotline for urgent situations — immediate connection to trained counselors any time of day.
