Leave Types at a Glance

Leave TypeWho It's ForKey Details
Flexible Time Off (FTO) Exempt (salaried) employees No accrual, no cap — take what you need with manager approval
Parental Leave New parents (birth, adoption, foster) Refer to policy for duration and pay details
Leave of Absence (LOA) Employees needing extended leave Medical, personal, or family reasons — requires HR approval
Paid Family & Medical Leave (PFML) Varies by state State-administered program for bonding, illness, or caregiving
Bereavement All employees Time off for loss of a family member; additional time via FTO
Holidays All employees Company-observed holidays listed in the handbook each year
