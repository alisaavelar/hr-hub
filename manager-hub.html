Manager Hub

1:1 guides, feedback frameworks, difficult conversation prep, new manager toolkit, and PIP support — everything managers need to lead well.
1 Skill Cowork Plugin

Why This Matters

People don't leave companies — they leave managers. And most managers were promoted because they were great individual contributors, not because they were trained to lead. This skill gives every manager a thought partner for the hard parts of the job: difficult conversations, performance concerns, 1:1s that actually help people grow, and navigating situations they've never faced before.

What This Skill Does

🤝
1:1 Frameworks
Structure effective 1:1s — agenda templates, question banks, how to balance operational check-ins with developmental conversations, and how to make them not feel like status updates.
💬
Feedback & Difficult Conversations
Prepare for hard conversations — underperformance, behavior issues, missed goals — with specific, non-punishing language that focuses on behavior and impact.
🆕
New Manager Toolkit
A structured 30/60/90-day guide for first-time managers — how to inherit a team, build trust, set expectations, and transition from IC to leader.

Manager Scenarios This Skill Handles

SituationWhat the Skill Produces
Underperformance conversationSpecific, non-accusatory language; SBI (Situation-Behavior-Impact) framing; next steps and follow-up plan
Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)PIP structure with goals, timelines, check-in cadence, and success criteria — fair, documented, and actionable
Team conflictFacilitation approach, mediation language, how to address the issue without taking sides
Managing upHow to escalate concerns, push back on direction, or ask for what the team needs from leadership
Inheriting a new teamFirst 30 days plan, relationship-building strategy, how to earn trust without pretending to have all the answers

Trigger Phrases

"Help me prepare for a difficult conversation with [name]" "Build a PIP for [name]" "I'm a new manager — what should I do in my first 30 days?" "Structure my 1:1s with my team" "How do I give feedback to someone who gets defensive?" "Help me address conflict between two team members"

Installation

⚡ Install the HR Hub Plugin

  1. Download hr-hub.plugin
  2. In Cowork, open Settings > Plugins
  3. Click Install from file and select the downloaded .plugin file
  4. All HR skills appear immediately. No restart needed.
