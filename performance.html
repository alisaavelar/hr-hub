Develop & Retain

Structured performance reviews and market-competitive comp — so your best people know where they stand and want to stay.
2 Skills Cowork Plugin Stage 03 of 04

Why This Matters

Retaining top talent comes down to two things: people feeling seen (performance reviews that are honest and developmental) and people feeling paid fairly (comp that reflects the market, not just the last raise). These skills handle both. Reviews stop being a blank-page problem, and comp conversations become data-backed rather than gut-feel.

Two Skills in This Stage

⭐
Performance Review
Self-assessment templates, manager review frameworks, calibration prep with rating distributions, and promotion cases — structured so every cycle runs smoothly.
💰
Comp Analysis
Benchmark any role against market data, build comp bands, model equity grants, and surface retention risks before they turn into resignation letters.

Performance Review: What Gets Built

📝
Self-assessment template. Structured prompts that help employees articulate their impact, development, and goals — not just a blank text box.
👤
Manager review template. Structured sections for performance vs. expectations, impact, growth areas, and a recommended rating — with guidance on turning vague feedback into specific behavioral examples.
⚖️
Calibration prep. Rating distribution summaries, promotion cases, and talking points for the calibration room — so you walk in prepared, not reactive.

Comp Analysis: What Gets Built

📊
Market benchmarking. Compare any role's comp against market data — P25, P50, P75 — so you know whether you're competitive, lagging, or leading.
🎯
Band placement. Check where a specific employee or candidate sits within your comp band, and whether an adjustment is warranted.
⚠️
Retention risk flags. Identify people who are likely underpaid relative to market — before they start looking externally.
