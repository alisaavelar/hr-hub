Recognition

Peer recognition, milestone celebrations, and awards programs — making people feel seen for the work they do.
1 Skill Cowork Plugin

Why This Matters

Recognition is one of the highest-leverage retention tools a people team has — and one of the most underfunded. Most managers want to recognize their people but don't know how to do it consistently, equitably, or memorably. This skill helps draft recognition messages, design programs, celebrate milestones, and build a culture where great work gets seen.

What This Skill Does

🏆
Recognition Messages
Draft specific, meaningful recognition for a team member — Slack shoutouts, all-hands callouts, LinkedIn recommendations, or award nominations.
🎂
Milestone Celebrations
Work anniversaries, promotions, major project completions — craft the right message for every milestone, at every tenure.
🎖️
Awards Programs
Design or refresh a recognition program — peer awards, quarterly spotlights, values-based recognition — with nomination templates and selection criteria.

Recognition Moments This Skill Handles

📣
Public shoutouts. Specific, non-generic Slack messages or all-hands callouts that name exactly what someone did and why it mattered.
🎉
Work anniversary messages. Tailored by tenure — a 1-year message hits differently than a 5-year one. Both should feel personal, not templated.
🚀
Promotion announcements. Draft the team-wide message that celebrates the promotion and tells the story of why this person earned it.
📝
Award nominations. Structure a compelling nomination for internal or external awards — with specific examples and impact language.

Trigger Phrases

"Write a recognition message for [name]" "[Name]'s 3-year work anniversary is coming up" "Draft a promotion announcement for [name]" "Help me design a quarterly awards program" "Nominate [name] for an award"
